DISCORD
BOT
My Python-built Discord bot is a feature-rich music bot that adds a dynamic dimension to any Discord server's music channel. It empowers users with a wide range of options, including queuing multiple songs, skipping tracks, and many more, to create an engaging and customizable music listening experience. With its versatility and interactive commands, this Python-powered bot enhances the entertainment value of Discord communities, making it a valuable addition for music enthusiasts.
YOUTUBE
DOWNLOADER
My Python app offers users a convenient way to download both videos and music from YouTube, providing a versatile and user-friendly solution for content enthusiasts. With its intuitive interface and robust functionality, users can effortlessly save their favorite videos and songs for offline enjoyment, making it a valuable tool for anyone looking to access YouTube content offline. This app enhances the overall YouTube experience by giving users the freedom to enjoy their media.
MOTTO
APP
The Motto mobile app offers users a unique motivational experience by providing one motto a day while also allowing them to access a different motto every time they seek inspiration. It empowers users to curate their favorite mottos, offering a customizable list for quick access, and with language options in both Polish and English, this app ensures users can connect with meaningful quotes in their preferred language, making it a versatile tool for daily inspiration.
HUMOR
APP
My humor app is a lighthearted tool that lets users track and visualize their daily mood with dynamic line charts and pie charts tailored to their emotions. Users can select their mood each day, generating a weekly overview that helps them understand their emotional patterns over time. Additionally, the app allows users to record and review the events and experiences that influenced their mood each day, providing valuable insights into their well-being and helping to bring a smile to their faces.
